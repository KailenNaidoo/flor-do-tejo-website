About Flor-Do-Tejo

Premium Products. Competitive Prices. Reliable Service.

Flor-Do-Tejo is a proudly local premium food supplier specializing in seafood, poultry, and frozen goods. We supply restaurants, hotels, and caterers across South Africa with hand-selected, ocean-fresh products and quality dry goods you can trust.

With a capacity of 700 tons monthly, we guarantee consistent quality and reliable delivery to keep your kitchen running at its best.
